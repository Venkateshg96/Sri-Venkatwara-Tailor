# Sri Venkatewara Tailor Website

A complete website for Sri Venkatewara Tailor Shop featuring all the sections and pages similar to the reference website.

## Features

### Main Website (index.html)
- **Header Navigation**: Fixed header with contact info, navigation menu, and action buttons
- **Hero Section**: Eye-catching banner with call-to-action buttons
- **Services Section**: 6 different tailoring services with icons and descriptions
- **Products Section**: Categorized product gallery with filtering
- **About Section**: Information about the business with features
- **Contact Section**: Contact form and business information
- **Account Section**: User profile management (hidden by default)
- **Orders Section**: Order tracking and history (hidden by default)
- **Footer**: Comprehensive footer with links and contact details

### Policy Pages
- **Privacy Policy** (privacy-policy.html)
- **Terms & Conditions** (terms-conditions.html)
- **Return & Refund Policy** (return-policy.html)
- **Shipping Policy** (shipping-policy.html)
- **Payment Policy** (payment-policy.html)

### Interactive Features
- **Mobile Responsive Design**: Works on all devices
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Direct contact via WhatsApp
- **Product Filtering**: Category-based product filtering
- **Contact Form**: Integrated with WhatsApp messaging
- **Cart System**: Basic shopping cart functionality
- **Smooth Scrolling**: Single-page navigation
- **Floating WhatsApp Button**: Always accessible contact

## How to Use

### For Customers:
1. **Browse Services**: Click on different service categories
2. **View Products**: Use category filters to find specific items
3. **Place Orders**: Click "Order Now" to contact via WhatsApp
4. **Contact**: Use the contact form or WhatsApp button
5. **Account**: Create account to track orders (feature ready)

### For Business Owner:
1. **Update Contact Info**: Modify phone numbers and addresses in all files
2. **Add Products**: Add new product items in the products section
3. **Update Prices**: Modify pricing in product cards
4. **Customize Services**: Edit services offered in the services section
5. **Policy Updates**: Modify policy pages as needed
