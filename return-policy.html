Return & Refund Policy

Effective Date: December 1, 2025

1. Custom Tailored Items

Due to the custom nature of our tailoring services, returns are generally not accepted for:

  • Custom tailored garments made to your measurements
  • Altered garments
  • Garments with personalized embroidery or modifications

2. Quality Issues

If you receive a garment with quality issues, we will:

  • Provide free alterations to fix the issue
  • Replace the garment if the issue cannot be fixed
  • Offer a full refund in extreme cases where neither repair nor replacement is satisfactory

3. Fit Issues

  • Free minor alterations within 7 days of delivery
  • Major fit issues will be assessed case by case
  • Additional charges may apply for significant modifications

4. Refund Process

Approved refunds will be processed:

  • Within 7-10 business days
  • To the original payment method
  • After deducting applicable charges if any

5. How to Request Return/Refund

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  • Provide order details and reason for return
  • Send photos if claiming quality issues
